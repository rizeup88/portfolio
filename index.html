What do I do for fun?

Outside of working on new designs, my interests are pretty broad, yet they keep me on my toes daily.

One of my biggest loves is the game of soccer, and cheering on the Portland Timbers here in my home city. Being a season ticket holder since 2012, I always look forward to game-days, and enjoying the fun of chanting and cheering with my friends in Green and Gold.

Still, when I'm not locked into work, I like to also get out and see the world by traveling. I've been to four different countries throughout my life, including traveling to France and Italy in 2019. After seeing the vast architecture, and iconic sights and spots, I knew I wanted to do more traveling in the future.

But to keep my creative side fueled, I rely on a big source of inspiration to do so, which is enjoying japanese animation (anime) and video games. With such intricate art and thirlling plot-lines to my favorite works, how could you not feel compelled to want to create memorable works with similar passion?
